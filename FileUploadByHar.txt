Pancit Canton is a delicious Filipino stir-fried noodle dish packed with meat, seafood, and vegetables. Here’s a simple way to cook it:

### Ingredients:
- 8 oz Pancit Canton noodles
- 2 cups chicken broth
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tbsp brown sugar
- 1 tbsp oil
- 1 lb pork tenderloin, sliced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 shallot, sliced
- 1 cup carrots, julienned
- 1 cup green beans, chopped
- 1 cup cabbage, shredded
- Salt and pepper to taste

### Instructions:
1. **Prepare the sauce**: Mix chicken broth, soy sauce, oyster sauce, and brown sugar in a bowl. Set aside.
2. **Cook the meat**: Heat oil in a wok or large skillet. Add pork and season with salt and pepper. Cook until browned.
3. **Sauté the vegetables**: Add garlic and shallots, then stir in carrots, green beans, and cabbage. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
4. **Add the noodles**: Pour in the seasoned broth and bring to a boil. Add Pancit Canton noodles, pressing them down to soak in the sauce.
5. **Finish and serve**: Stir and fold the noodles until all the liquid is absorbed and the noodles are tender. Adjust seasoning if needed. Serve immediately.
